OUR JOURNEY

Our Journey

A story of ambition, growth, and relentless innovation.

2022

SkillGrid Concept Created

The vision of a borderless freelance marketplace was born — sparked by the need for a more transparent and accessible platform.

2023

Platform Development Started

Our engineering and design teams came together to build the foundation of what would become SkillGrid's core marketplace.

2024

First 10,000 Registered Users

A major milestone — SkillGrid welcomed its first ten thousand freelancers and clients from 40+ countries worldwide.

2025

Global Freelancer Expansion

Expanded operations across 180+ countries with regional support, multi-currency payments, and localised onboarding flows.
